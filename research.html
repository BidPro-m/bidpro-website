Procurement Intelligence Research Programme

An independent research initiative examining procurement practices, digital transformation, technology adoption and innovation opportunities across South Africa.

Why this matters

Building reliable evidence about procurement.

Procurement is evolving through cloud technology, automation, analytics and artificial intelligence. Yet many organisations continue to manage opportunities, documentation and submission workflows through fragmented tools and manual processes.

This programme gathers practical evidence from organisations across South Africa to support industry benchmarking and the development of future procurement technology.

Research objectives

What the programme is designed to understand

01

Procurement challenges

Identify operational, administrative and compliance pain points.

02

Technology adoption

Understand current tools, workflows and digital maturity.

03

Future priorities

Assess feature demand, adoption intentions and pricing preferences.
